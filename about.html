Built on Faith & Family

For 22+ years, Gopala Media has been the trusted production partner for brands, creators, corporates, and storytellers across India. Follow our winding journey.

Scroll down to explore our story
  • 2002

    A Leap of Faith

    Gopala Media began in 2002, built on belief. After completing his law degree and working a decade in the video industry, Binod Gupta took the chance to start his own venture, supported by the unwavering strength and farmer-family grounded wisdom of his wife, Sangeeta.

  • The Name

    Why "Gopala"?

    The name "Gopala" represents two deeply personal blessings: their family devotion to Lord Krishna, and their love for their younger child, lovingly called Gopala at home. It became a promise to build something meaningful.

  • The Spark

    From One PD Camera

    We started with just one PD Camera. Placed with DD News—one of the biggest news channels at the time—through built goodwill, it marked our first crucial step. Long travel days, endless pitches, and pitches followed, but consistency prevailed.

  • Today

    22+ Years of Trust

    Today, Gopala Media is a full-scale production house. Having delivered 2,800+ productions, 1,500+ rentals, and serving 500+ corporate partners, we stand strong because of our faith, honesty, consistency, and family strength.

  • Partnerships

    Trusted by Global Brands

    Gopala Media is now the preferred production and gear partner for the world's leading brands. From executing high-profile commercial campaigns for BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and Flipkart, to supporting streaming production scales for Google and Netflix, we deliver world-class execution.
