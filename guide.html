1. Cinema Camera Architecture

Selecting the right sensor technology forms the visual backbone of your film. The ARRI Alexa Mini LF delivers industry-standard skin tones and organic highlight roll-off. RED V-Raptor 8K VV provides unmatched resolution for heavy VFX compositing, while the Sony Venice 2 excels in extreme low-light environments with dual native ISO.

2. High-Performance Optics & Anamorphic Lenses

Pairing sensors with optics dictates focus transition, flare character, and visual personality. Cooke S7/i Primes produce legendary warmth, Zeiss Supreme Primes offer clinical precision, and Sigma Cine Primes deliver fast T1.5 performance for night exteriors.

3. High-Output Lighting & Sound Rigging

Our lighting inventories combine Aputure 1200d Pro daylight punch with ARRI SkyPanel S60-C versatility. For location sound, Sound Devices 833 recorders paired with Sennheiser MKH-416 shotguns guarantee broadcast-ready dialogue even in noisy urban locations.
